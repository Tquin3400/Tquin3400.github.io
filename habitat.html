Boring Monologue

You need to arrest her, officer. I mean this Goldilocks person, she walked right into our house like it was hers. She ate the porridge that Mama Bear made for us. She was soooooo hungry. She could have just eaten one bowl but noooo she had to find the perfect one so she ate them all. And she did not even bring the dishes to the sink! Then she went to take a nap in our beds she messed all three of them up. Sat in all the chairs and broke one, and did not clean up. Who does this sort of thing?! I think she should have to pay a big fine. Actually, she probably does not care about what she did. Fining her is not enough, I want her in jail so she can think about her behavior! Yes, ARREST GOLDILOCKS! ARREST HER!
